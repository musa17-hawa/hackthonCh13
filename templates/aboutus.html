What We Do

Our goal, as students in the middle east, that understands the gaps between people, is to give our users the opportunity to get to know other cultures, to make new connections with new people around the world and to have interesting conversations in an free, easy, fun platform.

Why We Do It

We envision a world where people from different nationalities, countries and economical backgrounds can overcome the gaps and variations between them in order to ensure better relationships.
